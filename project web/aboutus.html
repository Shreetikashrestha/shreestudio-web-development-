About US

Shree Beauty & Makeup Studio: Redefining Glamour Since 2020

Shree Beauty & Makeup Studio was founded in 2020 by the visionary Shreetika Shrestha, a celebrated stylist and makeup artist with years of experience in the beauty industry. Shreetika honed her skills with advanced training from globally recognized academies and built a reputation for blending artistry with professionalism. Her journey in the beauty world has been defined by a relentless pursuit of excellence and an unyielding passion for empowering individuals through transformative beauty experiences.


After returning to Nepal, Shreetika identified a gap in the market—a need for a world-class beauty studio that could cater to the aspirations of Kathmandu’s diverse clientele, including urban trendsetters, celebrities, and professionals. Shree Beauty & Makeup Studio was born with the vision to deliver high-quality, personalized services that reflect international standards while embracing Nepal’s unique beauty culture.


A Journey of Excellence

What began as a humble endeavor in 2020 has quickly grown into one of Nepal’s most sought-after beauty destinations. Within just a few years, Shree Beauty & Makeup Studio earned a reputation for being a haven of elegance and sophistication. From bridal makeup to advanced skincare treatments, the studio offers a wide array of services tailored to meet the needs of modern individuals. At Shree Beauty, the team is what sets the studio apart. Carefully selected for their expertise and creativity, the stylists and makeup artists are internationally trained, bringing global techniques and trends to Nepal. Every member of the team is dedicated to providing an experience that is not just professional but also deeply personal.

Our Mission

At Shree Beauty & Makeup Studio, we believe that beauty is more than skin deep—it’s about embracing who you are and enhancing your confidence. We are dedicated to creating a space where artistry meets care, and every individual is empowered to shine in their own unique way.
